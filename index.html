How I tackle a project

Research

I research extensively about the company brand, the user's needs and the website requirements. Web app or website, I make sure to use the right tool and the right language to make sure that it works well on every device.

Design

I make my sketches and illustrations using a prototyping tool. I take great care about the responsiveness and the accessibility of my design. When ready, I export my finished prototype and I start putting those lines of code together.

Code

In my text editor of choice I choose the best language that fit my needs and I bring my ideas to life. Oh, and should other developers come across my projects: I ensure that the code is readable and well commented.
